Choripan

Ingredients

  • 8 sausages
  • 4 Marraquetas (a type of bread)
  • Chilean pebre
  • avocado cream
  • mayonaise
  • Chimichurri

How to cook

  1. Roast the sausages
  2. Make a cut in the center of marraquetas
  3. Put the roast sausages in the marraqueta
  4. Add the condiments you want
