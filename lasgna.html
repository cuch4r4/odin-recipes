Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 600 g beef for stew
  • 300 g pork belly
  • 1 cebolla
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 celery branch

How to cook

  1. Boil Or Soak Lasagna Noodles
  2. Make Meat Sauce
  3. Pour Tomato Sauce On The Bottom Of The Baking Dish
  4. Add A Layer Of Lasagna Noodles
  5. Add Another Layer Of Lasagna Noodles
  6. Add Tomato Sauce
  7. Add ricota cheese
  8. Add Another Layer Of Lasagna Noodles
  9. Add Tomato Sauce
  10. Sprinkle With Shredded Cheese
  11. Bake The Lasagna
